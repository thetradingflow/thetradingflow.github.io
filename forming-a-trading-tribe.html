Forming a trading tribe

One thing I miss from my time working at a hedge fund is the interactions with like-minded and smart individuals. Openly sharing ideas and research led to many interesting insights for everyone involved thanks to the different perspectives and skill sets people brought to the table.

I’d like to recreate a similar environment for a bunch of independent traders to get the same benefits minus the bullshit/constraints inherent to an investment firm. By sharing research and discussing ideas in a closed environment, with other traders we get to know and trust, chances are that we would all greatly benefit from it. We are small fishes in a huge ocean here, so the risk that we’re eating in each other’s plate is ridiculously low.

Who?

  • independent traders only: no compliance or NDA bullshit, thank you
  • must have experience in research/trading: industry experience not necessary, just experience doing it
  • must be trading with a solid capital base (> 100k USD) so we are generally operating in the same context
  • ideally working in Python to make it easier to collaborate
  • group of 3 to 7 people

How?

A private Discord server is neat for most interactions. All members would have equal rights and the server should be owned by all of us and not just by me. Add a GitHub repo for some code/data sharing. We’ll figure out the best way to get organized.

Purpose?

  • helping each other trading better and making more money
  • collaborating on common research projects
  • it’s not required to share everything, but all members must be contributing positively
  • getting feedbacks
  • having some fun

Nothing is written in stone obviously, these are just initial ideas.

If you’re interested and would like to get in touch, please send me a message on Twitter (@thetradler).
