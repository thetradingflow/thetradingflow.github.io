Anatomy of a trading system

A trading system is defined by 3 components:

  • an entry rule
  • a sizing rule
  • an exit rule

It is always:

  • unidirectional: long- or short-only
  • applied to a single asset
  • executed at no cost at the current bar’s closing price (ie, no delay)

The historical simulation of a trading system produces two outputs:

  • a time series of positions and P&Ls
  • a list of trades
