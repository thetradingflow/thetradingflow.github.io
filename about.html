About

I'm The Tradler - a weird combination of a trader and an idler.

I spent a decade working at quantitative investment firms in quant/PM roles, then left at 35 after reaching my financial goal to pursue my idleness aspirations along with my wife and our children.

As Tom Hodgkinson said:

Being idle is about being free [...]. It is about being free to live the lives we want to lead, free from bosses, wages, commuting, consuming and debt. Being idle is about fun, pleasure and joy.

As an idler, I live a simple life in a small village in Central Europe. When I'm not gardening, reading books, playing sports, improving my home or enjoying my time with my family, I can often be found hacking trading systems and coding in Python.

My trading approach is rather boring. With a low safe withdrawal rate (being frugal helps), I'm aiming first and foremost at maximizing the chances of sustaining my lifestyle. I have been running an intraday, fully automated trading program on digital assets for over two years with decent risk-adjusted performance on a sizeable account. I am now working on a fully automated trading program for futures (akin to a managed futures/CTA program) which should start live trading by the end of the summer 2022.

I share my thoughts and parts of my journey as an idle tradler on this website. Don't expect much innovative stuff though, I'm busy idling.
